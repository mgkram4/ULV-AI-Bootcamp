What You'll Learn

Our curriculum is designed to take you from foundational concepts to the state-of-the-art in AI.

Traditional AI

Explore the foundations of AI, including expert systems, knowledge representation, and classical problem-solving techniques.

Machine Learning

Understand supervised and unsupervised learning, decision trees, and how machines learn patterns from data.

Deep Learning

Dive into neural networks, backpropagation, and discover how deep learning revolutionized computer vision and NLP.

Transformer Architecture

Learn about the breakthrough attention mechanism that powers models like GPT and other state-of-the-art AI systems.

Natural Language Processing

Explore how AI understands and generates human language, from tokenization to advanced conversational AI.

AI Applications

Discover real-world applications of AI in healthcare, finance, autonomous vehicles, robotics, and emerging technologies.
